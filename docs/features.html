Features

Gashapon with buffs
For 2 points you get one random out of four buffs.
- Reduces damage taken by 15%
- Reduces bullet spread by 15%
- Increases damage dealt by 25%
- Increases movement speed by 10%
Drink machine (GREEN)
Allows you to restore 30% HP within 15 seconds for 3 points.
You get 1 point when an enemy dies.
Drink machine (RED)
Allows you to instantly restore 25% HP for 3 points.
You get 1 point when an enemy dies.
Jump
Some characters can jump over small obstacles.
Cover
Some characters can hide behind cover to avoid some bullet damage.
There are no official relationship with Nexon.
This game is not for commercial purpose.
The game uses assets from Blue Archive GLOBAL.
